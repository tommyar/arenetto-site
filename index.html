Arenetto

A refined three-row diatonic accordion for iPhone and iPad.

Arenetto is a focused digital instrument designed for responsive multitouch performance, authentic push-and-pull playing, multiple accordion registers, and configurable diatonic layouts.

Made for expressive playing

  • Three-row layoutsChoose between supported 31-button and 34-button diatonic arrangements.
  • Push and pullPlay distinct notes through responsive bellows-direction behavior.
  • Five registersMove between bassoon, bandoneon, master, violin, and clarinet voices.
  • Multitouch performancePlay chords and fast passages on a direct, landscape-oriented surface.
  • Flexible tuningsSelect from multiple diatonic tunings for different musical settings.
  • Configurable labelsShow note names and choose the accidental notation that suits you.
