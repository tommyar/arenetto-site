Privacy

Arenetto Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 22, 2026

This policy applies to Arenetto on iPhone, iPad, and Android, as well as this website.

Overview

Arenetto respects user privacy and is designed to operate without collecting personal information.

Data collection

Arenetto does not collect, transmit, sell, rent, or share personal information.

Arenetto does not use:

  • User accounts
  • Advertising
  • Analytics or tracking technologies
  • Marketing SDKs or third-party telemetry
  • Remote logging or cloud profiles
  • Location services, contacts, photos, or camera access
  • Microphone recording
  • Payment processing, in-app purchases, or subscriptions

Arenetto generates and plays musical audio. This audio playback does not involve recording through the device microphone.

Local preferences

Arenetto stores certain preferences locally on the device using storage provided by the device’s operating system. These preferences may include:

  • Selected tuning and register
  • Button count and button sizing
  • Note-label visibility
  • Accidental notation preference
  • Other interface configuration

These preferences remain on the device, are not transmitted to Arenetto, and are not shared with third parties. They can generally be removed by deleting the app. Backup behavior is controlled by the user’s Apple, Google, and operating-system settings.

Network use and website hosting

Arenetto does not require an account or a connection to Arenetto-operated servers for normal instrument operation.

Opening the Privacy Policy or Support page may cause the user’s browser to connect to the website host. Standard web-server records may temporarily contain technical request information such as an IP address, timestamp, requested page, and browser user-agent.

The Arenetto website is hosted using GitHub Pages. GitHub may process limited technical information necessary to deliver the website in accordance with GitHub’s own privacy practices.

Arenetto does not add analytics, advertising, tracking scripts, cookies, or other third-party scripts to this website.

Children’s privacy

Arenetto does not knowingly collect personal information from children or from any other users.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated when the app or applicable legal requirements change. When it is updated, the effective date shown at the top of this page will also be updated.

Contact

For privacy questions, email support@arenetto.app.
